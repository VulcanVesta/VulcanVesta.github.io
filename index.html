First post

23 January 2023

Something exciting happened today. Nothing. Today was an undeniably normal day, which makes it very unique. I want more days to be like this. I want to drink tea while casually doing homework after a boring day of school. I want only my best friend to send me a funny video and continuing on after a chuckle. I want to watch a mediocre rom-com and fall asleep at 10.
These kinds of days will become more and more common as I grow old. But I don't want them to be every day. You need gray between rainbow colours. I don't want an arrogant fantasy nor a dull reality. I want to live life as it is. I want to live sandwhiched between heaven and hell.

Second post

29 January 2023

The year is long and life is even longer. One may think that's a complaint, but really it just depends on the day I'm having. Time links intimately with patience. When a period seems long, your patience grows thin and it seems even longer. The opposite is also true.
I wish time was constant. I wish we could be honest to ourselves when we say, "oh when will it end!" and I wish we wouldn't ever have to think those dreadful words, "wait, it's over already?".
